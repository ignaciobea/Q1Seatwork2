CHARACTERS IN NOLI ME TANGERE

CHARACTERS DESCRIPTION

Don Rafael		 Father of Juan Crisostomo Ibarra. He was accused to have instigated murder and not confessing in to the priests. Died in jail, without justice.

Crisostomo Ibarra		 Don Rafael Ibarra's offspring. Was raised in Sandiego and became good friends with maria Clara, Kapitan Tiago's daughter. He went to Europe to study, and stayed there in the span of seven years. Ibarra was an ideal man to many, he was well brought up, studied abroad, and was knowledgable. Very much envied, too.

Maria Clara		 Ibarra's childhood friend and the woman he was bound to marry. Their marriage was arraged, but feelings were certainly not. A woman in their time, often seen objective, inevitably harassed.

Tenyente Guevarra		 An old friend of Don Rafael Ibarra's and a lieutenant of the Guardia Civil. He informed Crisostomo Ibarra of his father's death and why.
